I can't run the program...

It is a common issue, and if it is a Windows Protected Your PC error, then it's very simple to fix it. Click on More Info and click on Run Anyway. Why is this happening? Is this Virus? No, I can't even make viruses since I don't have enough knowledge for that. Simply it's a rare type of program as this language is very old, plus it's a Console application. These are ran by your CMD (Command Prompt), but don't worry, navigation is easy and simple, even though there's no mouse involved.
